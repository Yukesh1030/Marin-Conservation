Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our programs, donations, and volunteer logistics.

How are donations utilized?
85% of our funding directly goes to active ocean restoration campaigns, clinics operations, patrol boat fuels, and sensor deployment. The remaining 15% covers administrative services, staff, and website upkeep.
Can I volunteer without marine biology experience?
Yes, absolutely! Our beach plastic cleanups, sorting sessions, and local webinars require no scientific background. We provide all necessary on-site safety guides and instructions when you arrive.
What is a Marine Protected Area (MPA)?
An MPA is an ocean zone designated by governments where human activities, particularly commercial transit, waste disposal, oil drilling, and commercial fishing are legally restricted to allow ecosystem recovery.
How does satellite migration tracking work?
We tag animals using non-invasive suction-cup transmitters. When the animal breaks the ocean surface, the antenna signals orbiting weather satellites, transmitting location coordinates, dive depths, and water temperatures.
