Featured Marine Life

Meet some of the spectacular species we are working tirelessly to protect and keep safe from extinction.

Sea Turtle

Ancient navigators facing severe threats from ocean plastic pollution and coastal nesting beach degradation.

Blue Whale

The largest animal ever to exist, slowly recovering in vast protected ocean sanctuaries.

Dolphin

Highly intelligent social creatures protected from industrial net entanglement and ocean noise.

Coral Reefs

Diverse marine nurseries sheltering 25% of ocean species, being actively restored against bleaching.

Our Mission

We design and deliver high-impact conservation projects that restore ocean habitats, protect vulnerable marine wildlife, and secure sustainable fisheries. Working alongside local communities, scientists, and governments, we target key areas that make a significant difference.

  • 100% science-backed ecological restoration frameworks.
  • Empowering coastal communities for sustainable livelihoods.
  • Lobbying for global policies to protect high seas zones.

Conservation Programs

Through our strategic initiatives, we address the root causes of marine degradation and restore hope.

Coral restoration

Growing climate-resilient corals in land nurseries and planting them to restore degraded structures.

Success Stories

Look back at the key milestones we have achieved in our mission to repair and protect the blue planet.

2024

15,000 Hectares Protected

Secured government policies declaring deep-sea canyons off the Atlantic coast as a no-take marine sanctuary.

2025

Coral Nursery Expansion

Successfully expanded our nursery facilities in Hawaii, transplanting 10,000 heat-resistant coral fragments.

2026

Global Ocean Pact

Lobbied and drafted key clauses of the binding high-seas plastic restriction treaty signed by 80 coastal states.
